How to register for courses.

Follow the below steps for a successful application

Sign Up

Click on sign up and fill all the relevant fields (if not already have account). Upon successful sign up, sign in to your portal.

Complete the form

Click on complete admission form to completely enter your details.

Make Payment

Click on "Make Payment" to pay admission fee and other charges.

Print Receipt

After successful payment, click on the print button to print you payment receipt which you will bring along for the screen exercise.
